Bedrock Modules¶

DSP¶

In the DSP directory you cna find various various digital signal processing algorithms implemented in platform-independent (portable) Verilog, and their test benches; modules include DDS, Down-conversion, Up-conversion, CIC Filters, Low-Pass filters, High-Pass filters, Mixers.
